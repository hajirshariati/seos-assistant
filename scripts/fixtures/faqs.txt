SHIPPING & DELIVERY

Q: Do you offer free shipping?
A: Free standard ground shipping on all U.S. orders, no minimum.

Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Online orders are processed and shipped within 48 hours. Depending on
   destination, U.S. estimated delivery is typically 3–7 business days from
   purchase date. Orders do not ship on Saturdays, Sundays, or holidays.

Q: Do you offer expedited shipping?
A: Yes, expedited and overnight available at checkout for a fee.

Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: Aetrex now ships worldwide. International shipping times and costs vary by
   region and are shown during checkout.

Q: How do I track my order?
A: For specific order tracking, the support team can pull it up.

RETURNS & EXCHANGES

Q: What's your return policy?
A: Wear your Aetrex shoes or orthotics for up to 30 days from the date your
   order was received. If they are not the perfect fit, send them back through
   the return portal.

Q: Are returns free?
A: A $5.95 return fee is automatically deducted when processing U.S. returns.
   International return shipping costs vary by country and carrier and
   typically start at approximately $20 USD.

Q: How do I start a return?
A: Visit returns.aetrex.com, enter your order details, choose the item and
   reason, then select your return method. U.S. customers can use either the
   QR-code return option or a prepaid return label.

Q: When will I get my refund?
A: The refund is processed to the original form of payment and should be
   reflected in your account within 5–7 days from the day of drop-off.

Q: Are final sale items returnable?
A: Styles discounted 60% off or more are final sale. Products marked Final Sale
   are non-refundable and cannot be returned.

Q: Can I partially return the Plantar Fasciitis Kit?
A: No. The Plantar Fasciitis Kit cannot be partially returned. All elements
   within the kit — orthotic, roller, flips, and guide — must be returned
   together, and products cannot be swapped for other colors or SKUs.

Q: Damaged or wrong item?
A: Handled by the support team — they'll make it right.

SIZING & FIT

Q: How do Aetrex shoes fit?
A: Most styles run true to size.

Q: Can I trim Aetrex orthotics?
A: No — Aetrex Orthotics are NOT trimmable. Pick the size matching
   your usual shoe size.

Q: My shoes don't have a removable insole.
A: Look at the Thinsoles line.

ORTHOTIC BASICS

Q: How long do Aetrex orthotics last?
A: Most customers replace every 6–12 months.

Q: How do I clean orthotics?
A: Wipe with a damp cloth and mild soap, then air-dry. Don't
   machine-wash or put them in a dryer.

Q: Are these the same as Lynco?
A: They're now branded as Aetrex Orthotics with Aetrex Signature
   Arch Support. Same engineering, current name.

Q: Plantar fasciitis?
A: Aetrex carries a Plantar Fasciitis Kit specifically for that.

Q: Kids orthotics?
A: Yes — Kids Orthotics line.
